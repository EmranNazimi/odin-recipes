Buttered Noodles

What is this Awesome looking Buttered Noodles?

Simple to prepare, saucy, buttered noodles are excellent on their own or with steak, chicken, or meatballs. For this quick and simple, kid-friendly dinner, all you need is butter, Parmesan cheese, salt, and pepper, however you could also add fresh herbs and a little lemon juice to enhance the flavor.

Ingredients

  • 1 (16 ounce) package fettuccine noodles
  • 6 tablespoons butter, cut into pieces
  • â…“ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Begin by boiling a large pot of salted water. Once it is boiling, add the noodles of your choice and cook them until they are al dente, about 8-10 minutes.
  2. Drain the noodles and set aside
  3. In the same pot you used to cook the noodles, melt the butter over medium-low heat.
  4. Add the cooked noodles back to the pot and stir until they are evenly coated with the butter.
  5. Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese,salt, and pepper, stirring to combine.
  6. Taste and adjust seasonings as desired.
  7. Serve immediately and enjoy!
